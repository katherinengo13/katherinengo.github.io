UI/UX, Design, UX Research

Duration: 10 weeks

Role: UX Designer, UX Researcher

How might we show that Dexcom cares for users at all points in the troubleshooting journey?

Background

For 10 weeks, I was a UX Researcher and Designer for the Diabetes Design Initiative, which is under the Design Lab at UC San Diego. We worked directly with Dexcom, a diabetes device company, to help improve the user experience. I spent the entire quarter working on a project that aimed to improve the trouble shooting experience for Dexcom CGM users.

Experience

Through this experience, I took on many tasks in order to successfully go through the design process and create a final presentation for the Design Lab and Dexcom mentors. Some tasks include:

  • Running user interviews

  • Secondary research

  • Prototyping

  • Journey mapping

  • Conducting user tests

  • Running workshops with mentors

  • Creating presentations
