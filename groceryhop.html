Personal Project

UI/UX, Design, UX Research

Duration: 8 weeks

Role: UI/UX Designer, UX Researcher, Visual Designer

How might we help people efficiently grocery shop in order to ensure their safety and sanitation during the COVID-19 pandemic?

Background

Currently, the only time people leave their homes is to go grocery shopping, where people must shop with caution in order to maintain social distancing. Grocery shopping is essential, but also risky because people go out in public during a time when it is encouraged to stay inside our homes. I decided to work on creating a grocery shopping app that aims to help individuals shop efficiently and safely during this pandemic.

The COVID-19 pandemic has quickly changed everyone’s lives. Grocery shopping has become the only time people leave their homes, but it is a difficult and intimidating task. People must be in public with other individuals and touch items that strangers have touched. Although there is social distancing - the task of grocery shopping has become an anxious and stressful experience for everyone. Individuals should be able to shop efficiently and safely. People should be able to get in and out of grocery stores quickly, while still gathering enough the items they need.

Interviews

I conducted interviews to learn about people’s grocery shopping experiences from before and after the pandemic. Here are some important takeaways from the interviews.

Takeaways

  • People buy groceries they need by section/category

  • People are more anxious now than before

  • Grocery stores are now a tense and stressful environment

  • Grocery shopping needs to be a quick and easy task

  • Important items are often out of stock due to people panic buying

Personas

I created two different personas - a college student and a father. This allowed to work on creating an app that can cater to different types of users.

Amanda

A College Student.

Worries

  • Getting/giving COVID-19 at the grocery store

  • The stressful and tense environment at the grocery store

  • Not having the right groceries for her daily meals

Groceries Shopping Background

  • Buys ingredients for her daily meals but also to try out new recipes

  • Saves recipes and opens them up at the store

  • Doesn't use an organized list

Shopping Goals

  • Buy what she needs, but also buy a variety of ingredients to experiment with recipes

  • Get in and out of the grocery store efficiently

Dan

A Husband and Father.

Worries

  • Getting/giving COVID-19 at the grocery store

  • Giving COVD-19 to his family

  • Not having enough groceries to feed his family

Groceries Shopping Background

  • Shops for his family

  • Writes down his grocery list on a piece of paper

Shopping Goals

  • Buy enough groceries to last about 2 weeks

  • Provide enough food for his family

Storyboards

Creating storyboards helped me place the personas into specific situations and allowed me to look at the user journey.

Competitive Analysis

I downloaded multiple grocery apps in order to learn more about the strengths and weaknesses of other grocery shopping apps.

Design Goals

  • Features for efficient shopping

  • Useful store information

  • Simple and relaxing design

  • Intuitive user interface

Final Product


Current Lists


Users can quickly access their current lists


Lists are grouped into categories and sorted by priority


When a user checks off an item, the items automatically moves to the bottom of the list to ensure efficient shopping.

Adding a New List


When a user creates a list, a pop up will ask if they would like to add items they never got in their last list


Users can quickly create the list - where they have the option to put in the category, quantity, and priority


Feedback for the user when they finish creating a list

Your Recipes


Users can access a page with their saved recipes


A user can quickly add the ingredients of a recipe to any of their list(or a new list)


Recipes can be added through importing a web recipe, or writing their own

Grocery Stores


Users can see their nearby and saved grocery stores
